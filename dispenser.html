  • Dispenser

  • Chips are inserted through slots in the back and then stored inside tubes ready to be dispensed. Each tube can hold up to 10 chips.

    • The chip quantity is recorded in a database

  • Three servos mounted side-by-side move racks that release red, green and blue chips.

  • The three servos run simultaneously back and forth until they have dispensed the required number of chips.

  • Dispensed chips are caught in a container that sits behind a closed hatch.

    • Chip container located on slider for easy retrieval.
